# 🏦 Providus Bank × Unity Bank Merger Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes the merger between Providus Bank and Unity Bank, providing executives with a comprehensive view of financial performance, customer metrics, geographic coverage, digital banking adoption, and loan portfolio insights.

This project demonstrates how business intelligence can support strategic decision-making during large-scale organizational mergers.

---

## 📖 Project Overview

Bank mergers involve integrating customers, branches, financial assets, and operational systems while maintaining business performance. Without a centralized reporting system, executives may struggle to monitor the success of the integration.

This dashboard consolidates key performance indicators into a single interactive report, enabling stakeholders to track the health of the merged institution, compare performance across business areas, and make informed decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The dashboard was built to answer key business questions such as:

- How is the merged bank performing financially?
- What is the total customer and account base?
- Which regions have the strongest banking presence?
- How are customers adopting digital banking channels?
- What does the loan portfolio look like after the merger?
- How can management monitor post-merger performance effectively?

# 📈 Business Insights

From the dashboard, several business insights emerge:

- The merged bank has established a strong nationwide footprint with **229 operational branches**.
- The institution manages a large customer base supported by thousands of active accounts.
- Digital banking channels such as Mobile Banking and USSD continue to drive customer engagement.
- Geographic analysis reveals differences in customer concentration and financial performance across regions.
- Financial KPIs enable executives to monitor organizational performance after the merger.
- Loan portfolio monitoring provides visibility into lending performance and potential risks.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations can help improve business performance:

- Continue investing in digital banking services to improve customer experience.
- Expand marketing initiatives in regions with lower customer penetration.
- Monitor branch performance regularly to optimize operational efficiency.
- Strengthen customer retention strategies throughout the post-merger integration period.
- Closely monitor loan portfolio quality to reduce credit risk.
- Use executive dashboards to support faster, data-driven decision-making.
